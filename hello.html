P OEM 4

In the darkest winter of my life,
When all that I could see was strife;
I faithfully followed a vibrant light,
That stayed me through the starless night.

My heart did spring from that icy chill;
When I think of it, I can feel it still.
I've only just begun to heal
I 've only just begun to feel...

The sooting breeze summer waves,
W hen every day is sunny day;
It's funny, I can finally say
It feels good to be alive today!

Though every now and then I fall,
I'm losing leaves, but through it all,
Through the darkest winters of my life;
I never lost that vibrant light.
